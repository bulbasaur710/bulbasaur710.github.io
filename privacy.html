Soap Slicer ASMR stores gameplay data (statistics, badges, streaks, preferences) locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. This data is NOT transmitted to any external server.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA when authorized), IP address, general location data, and app usage data. You can opt out of personalized ads through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in the Advertising section.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally. Uninstalling the App deletes all stored data.
